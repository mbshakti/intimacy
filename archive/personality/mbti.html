Myers-Briggs Type Indicator

A framework that categorizes personality across four dichotomies, producing 16 possible types. Based on Carl Jung's theory of psychological types, developed by Isabel Briggs Myers and Katharine Cook Briggs.

The Four Axes

E / IExtraversion vs Introversion — where you direct energy. Outward toward people and action, or inward toward ideas and reflection.
S / NSensing vs Intuition — how you take in information. Concrete facts and present reality, or patterns, possibilities, and the abstract.
T / FThinking vs Feeling — how you make decisions. Logic and objective criteria, or values, relationships, and emotional impact.
J / PJudging vs Perceiving — how you deal with the world. Preferring structure and decisions, or flexibility and keeping options open.
